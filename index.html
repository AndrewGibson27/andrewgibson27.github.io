Andrew Gibson

I’m an Austin-based scrum master working at Charles Schwab. I hold Certified ScrumMaster and Certified Associate in Project Management credentials.

Previously, I worked as a senior front-end engineer at The Texas Tribune, a nonprofit news organization focusing on politics and policy. I helped build, maintain and improve in-house apps and sites. This included the core website and CMS, donation platform, user portal and pixel tracker. I focused primarily on JavaScript. I also led sprint plannings, helped maintain the engineering backlog and managed third-party development of the Texas Tribune Festival website.

Before that, I worked at the Orlando Sentinel. I built interactive projects, maps, databases, graphics and quizzes. I was also the core developer in charge of delivering election results for the Central Florida and the Fort Lauderdale areas.

For about two years, I was co-organizer of the Austin Web Performance meetup. I’ve also helped organize a few hackathons (this one and this one).

I’ve spoken at the International Symposium for Online Journalism in Austin, the National High School Journalism Convention in D.C., and News Xchange in Marrakesh, Morocco.

I’m a Missouri School of Journalism alumnus and native of the Denver area. Go Broncos.
